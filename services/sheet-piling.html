SHEET PILING AND SHORING

DEEPAM ENGINEERING provides the sheet pilling & shoring services for the construction purpose to our clients. Steel sheet piles walls are commonly installed for shallow excavations where the ease of driving a series of steel sheets using excavator mounted vibro-hammer for a very economical soil retention system. Our excavator equipped with a specially fabricated dipper arm extension and a vibro-hammer has the ability to install steel sheet piles at 6m and 12m long.

The vibro-hammer drives the sheet pile using high frequency vibrations that liquefies most granular soils. This vibrational force coupled with the down-crowd force applied by excavator boom coupled with weight of vibro-hammer and the pile is sufficient to drive in ground . The vibro-hammer works equally well in reverse for pile extraction.
