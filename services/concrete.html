CONCRETE CRUSHING & RECYCLING

With strict landfill regulations and increasing fuel prices DEEPAM ENGINEERING introduced concrete crushing and recycling services to our valuable clients.Once a building has been dismantled or demolished we recycle the concrete aggregates, using them to backfill areas on sites or we sell the aggregates to be re-used elsewhere. In demolition sites produce thousands of pounds of concrete. With strict landfill regulations and increasing fuel prices, Whether you need to tear down a building or have concrete from a previously demolished structure, you can count us to keep your project moving in the right direction.



Concrete crushing is a process in which we grind concrete into gravel that you can reuse and recycle. Before our DEEPAM ENGINEERING experts can crush concrete, they tear down the respective building using precision equipment, such as high-reach excavators with attachments. We then separate the concrete, place it in a discrete location at your site, and remove debris.

An excavator then loads the concrete into the crusher, which has strong interlocking teeth that smash the blocks into small pieces. After processing the concrete, we sort the pieces by size and use an electromagnet to remove metal that you can recycle or sell.
